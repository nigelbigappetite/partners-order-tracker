08 — Next Week Focus

What the kitchen takes into next week.

01

Reduce Deliveroo prep time

Target 20-25 minutes: start each order promptly, keep cooked chicken held hot above 63°C, and complete the pack check before the rider arrives.

02

Prep for Friday and Saturday peaks

Before 17:00, confirm wraps, loaded fries components, wings, tenders, dips and packaging are stocked at the station; Friday was the biggest day at £638.

03

Tighten dispatch checks

Before sealing each bag, check the ticket against every item, dip, drink and modifier; the order should leave complete the first time.

04

Protect the winning items

Smokey BBQ Chicken Wrap and Banging Buffalo Loaded Fries are the demand drivers; check sauce amount, garnish, fries and wrap stock before every peak.
