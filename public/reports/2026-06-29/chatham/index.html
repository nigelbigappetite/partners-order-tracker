08 — Next Week Focus

What the kitchen takes into next week.

01

Protect wrap build quality

Build wraps tight enough to travel: balanced filling, sauce controlled, wrap closed cleanly, and packaging checked before sealing.

02

Keep food hot through dispatch

Cooked chicken must leave hot: hold above 63°C after cooking, pack immediately when complete, and do not leave finished food waiting open on the pass.

03

Reduce Deliveroo prep time

Target 20-25 minutes: start each order promptly, assign ticket ownership, and finish the pack check before the rider arrives.

04

Final bag check every order

Before sealing, check the receipt against every main, side, dip, drink and modifier so the order leaves complete the first time.
