08 — Next Week Focus

What the kitchen takes into next week.

01

Reduce Deliveroo prep time

Target 20-25 minutes: start high-volume items as soon as the order lands and keep cooked chicken held hot above 63°C until packed.

02

Prep for Friday and Saturday peaks

Pre-load wraps, loaded fries components, wings, tenders, dips and packaging before 17:00; Friday was the biggest day at £638.

03

Keep ads on only when the line is ready

Deliveroo ads returned 3.50x, so keep them active, but use busy mode before prep exceeds 30 minutes.

04

Protect the winning items

Smokey BBQ Chicken Wrap and Banging Buffalo Loaded Fries are the demand drivers; check sauce, garnish, fries and wrap stock before every peak.
