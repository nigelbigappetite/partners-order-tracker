08 — next week focus

What the kitchen takes into next week.

01

Cooked chicken standard: probe before packing

Probe the thickest piece of chicken before it leaves the kitchen: core temperature must read 75°C or above. If it is below 75°C, keep cooking and do not pack the order.

02

Packing standard: final bag check on every Uber order

Before sealing the bag, read the receipt out loud and tick off every main, side, dip, drink and modifier. Two Uber claims were missing-item issues, so no bag leaves without this check.

03

Peak prep standard: build for Wednesday and Friday

Wednesday hit £531 and Friday hit £469 with 18 orders. Pre-prep extra wings, loaded fries components, bundles and sandwich fillets before the dinner rush so speed does not drop when demand spikes.
