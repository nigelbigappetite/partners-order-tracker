08 — next week focus

What the kitchen takes into next week.

01

Keep Mac N Cheese Bites hot and fresh

32 sold this week — the highest volume item on Uber. One customer flagged cold/melted in their item rating. Check serving temperature and packaging before every dispatch.

02

Reduce Uber courier wait and order duration

Average courier wait was 7.93 mins with 3.04 mins avoidable. Total order duration averaged 47.86 mins. Uber low ratings mentioned slow delivery directly. Tightening dispatch timing will protect the rating.

03

Stock up for Wed, Fri, Sat peaks

Wednesday was the peak sales day at £210. Friday and Saturday ran 7 orders each — the busiest kitchen days of the week. Run higher stock on Mac N Cheese Bites, Loaded Fries, and bundle components on these three days.

04

Keep Banging Buffalo Loaded Fries consistent

24 sold across both platforms — the cross-platform hit. One 1-star item rating, though tagged "good portion" — the issue is likely delivery temperature, not the item. Watch packaging and timing.
